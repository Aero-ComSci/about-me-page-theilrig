Awoke News Site November - 2023 Docu Link

Objective was to create a project of our design. The final product ended up becoming a news site. This site has 2 main tags linking to articles by other sources including the BBC and NYC Times on conflicts in Israel and Venezuela. In addition it features the “Awoke” branding and has its own introductory paragraph. Documentation wise it was made with HTML, CSS and additional design elements from Canva.

Cat in the Hat Touch Typing May - 2024 Docu Link

Objective was to create a product capable of solving problems. The problem in question was helping with youth dyslexia and preparing them for the future. The solution was to create a touch typing game similar to other software products. It would go letter by letter until the entirety of Dr.Seuss’s The Cat in the Hat was completed. And has an on screen timer system tracking time left and how many chances remain for the player. All developed in pygame.
